Welcome to FixMyCity – Empowering Citizens to Enhance City Infrastructure

FixMyCity is a platform that connects citizens, contractors, and government authorities to improve urban maintenance through efficient reporting, collaboration, and transparency. Together, we can make cities cleaner, safer, and better for everyone.

For Citizens

Take Action in Your Community Easily report urban issues, from potholes to waste disposal, and track their resolution. Your contributions are rewarded and make a tangible impact on your city.

For Contractors:

Get access to real urban maintenance jobs assigned by authorities, increase your credibility with user ratings, and get paid upon successful completion.

For Government Authorities:

Efficiently oversee urban issues, allocate contractors, and track progress on reported issues, all from one platform.

Community-driven

Empower citizens to take charge of their city’s infrastructure through active involvement.

Collaboration:

Seamless communication between citizens, contractors, and authorities ensures fast and effective problem-solving.

Transparency:

All processes, from reporting to contractor ratings, are fully transparent for all users.
